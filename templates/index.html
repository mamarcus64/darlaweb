Dartmouth Linguistic Automation

DARLA is a suite of vowel formant extraction tools tailored to research questions in sociophonetics.

Completely Automated Vowel Extraction

Audio → Formants

This automated method is designed for "big data" research projects where a general vowel space is prioritized over word-level transcription accuracy.

See details »

Semi-Automated Alignment and Extraction

Audio + Transcriptions → Formants

Equivalent to the FAVE pipeline, with additional input and output options. Designed for research goals that require very accurate transcriptions.

See details »

System Output

  1. Aligned TextGrids
  2. Formant plots representing the speakers' vowel space
  3. Vowel formant measurements
  4. Probabilities for the phonetic environments of each vowel token
  5. Options for manually correcting the transcriptions and alignments
